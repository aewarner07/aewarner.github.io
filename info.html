The Infographic project was intended to describe a type of communication technology. It highlights the origin, operating principle, uses, and impacts, in this case it's streaming. This infographic shares a brief history, popular streaming services, how it works, and the pros and cons. The infographic itself was created on Adobe Indesign. Images and information were sourced from google. The graphics were provided by an artist, Charlie Gordon, on The Noun Project website.
